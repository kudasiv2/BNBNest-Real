Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our investment platform

What is the minimum deposit?

The minimum deposit is 10 USDT to start earning with BNBNest

How does the ROI system work?

ROI ranges from 0.5% to 2.5% daily based on the total TVL. The more TVL grows, the higher the ROI percentage

What is the lock period for capital?

Your capital is locked for 28 days. After this period, you can withdraw your capital with a 10% fee

How do referral commissions work?

You earn commissions from 17 levels of your referral network, with percentages ranging from 2% to 20% based on your deposit tier

How can I qualify for monthly salary?

Monthly salary tiers require specific deposit amounts and referral counts at different levels. Check the Salary section for details
